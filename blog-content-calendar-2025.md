## SEO Strategy & Keyword Research Methodology

### Primary Keyword Selection Criteria
- **Commercial Intent**: B2B search terms from repair professionals
- **Search Volume**: 500+ monthly searches for sustainable traffic
- **Competition Level**: Difficulty scores 50-75 for achievable rankings
- **Business Value**: Keywords that convert to wholesale leads
- **Long-tail Focus**: Specific product/model combinations

### Difficulty Score Interpretation
- **50-60**: Easy to rank (low competition, establish quick wins)
- **61-70**: Medium difficulty (balanced opportunity/cost)
- **71-80**: Hard but achievable (high-value targets requiring effort)

### Seasonal Timing Strategy
- **Q1 (Jan-Mar)**: Pre-season planning, tool/equipment focus
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun)**: Peak season preparation, part availability
- **Q3-Q4**: Market analysis, trend forecasting (not covered in 6-month calendar)

## Content Optimization Guidelines

### On-Page SEO Requirements
- **Title Tag**: 50-60 characters, primary keyword first
- **Meta Description**: 150-160 characters with benefits and trust signals
- **URL Structure**: `/blog/[category]/[target-keyword]/`
- **H1**: Exact match primary keyword
- **H2/H3**: Supporting keywords, user intent focused
- **Word Count**: 1,500-2,500 words for comprehensive authority

### Content Structure Template
```markdown
# [Primary Keyword] - [Value Proposition]

## Introduction (200 words)
- Hook with industry statistic
- Problem statement for repair shops
- Solution preview with Midas positioning

## [Section H2] (400 words)
- Detailed analysis/comparison/tutorial
- Data-driven insights
- Practical applications

## [Section H2] (400 words)
- Alternative perspectives
- Real-world examples
- Technical specifications

## [Section H2] (300 words)
- Best practices/recommendations
- Wholesale benefits emphasis
- Midas product integration

## Conclusion (200 words)
- Key takeaways summary
- Call-to-action for wholesale application
- Contact information and next steps
```

### Internal Linking Strategy
- **Homepage**: 2 links per article (header, footer)
- **Category Pages**: 3-4 links (related products, "Shop Now" CTAs)
- **Product Pages**: 2 links (specific part recommendations)
- **Other Blog Posts**: 3-5 contextual links (related guides)
- **Wholesale Application**: 1 prominent CTA link
